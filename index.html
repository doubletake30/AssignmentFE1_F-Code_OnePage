About Us

F-Code Club

F-Code , established in 2014, is the first academic club of FPT University at Ho Chi Minh City. With the initial aim of creating a community for Software Engineering major’s students, F-Code allows them to share and improve skills that are neccesary for developers. After 5 years, F-Code has offered various activities to students.
